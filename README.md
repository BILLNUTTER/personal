# My Life Journey Portfolio

Welcome to my personal portfolio website! This website showcases my journey, including childhood experiences, education, skills, projects, and future aspirations in software development.

**Website link:** [personalcalvin.netlify.app](https://personalcalvin.netlify.app)

## About Me

Hi, I am **Calvin**, a Bachelor of Science in **Software Engineering** student at **Kirinyaga University**. This portfolio highlights my academic growth, technical skills, and professional goals.

## Features

* **Home / Landing Page:** Overview of my journey and sections to explore.
* **Childhood:** Early life experiences, school life, and formative moments.
* **Education:** Academic achievements, learning experiences, and competitions.
* **Skills:** Technical abilities, problem-solving skills, and collaborative experiences.
* **Future Aspirations:** Career goals and long-term vision in software engineering.
* **Contact:** Easy ways to reach me via email, X (Twitter), Instagram, and WhatsApp.

## Technologies Used

* **HTML5** – for the structure of the web pages.
* **CSS3** – for styling and layout, including modern gradient and hover effects.
* **JavaScript** – for navigation between pages and interactive elements.

## How to Navigate

* Use the **navigation bar** at the top to move between pages.
* Click the **“Explore the Journey”** button on the landing page to start exploring my life journey.
* Access my contact links directly from the **Contact** page.
